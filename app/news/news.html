Tetris.js 06 Oct 15:05 Add statistics about number of lines cleared

Now besides t.score you can use t.lines to see how many times you cleared 1,2,3 or 4 lines at a time.

  • t.lines[0] - how many pieces were placed without clearing any line
  • t.lines[1] - how many times you cleared 1 row
  • t.lines[2] - how many times you cleared 2 rows
  • t.lines[3] - how many times you cleared 3 rows
  • t.lines[4] - how many times you cleared 4 rows

Simulator 04 Oct 19:40 Add Play/Pause/Step/Jump buttons to simulator

Allow pausing, playing step by step and jumping ahead in the simulator. Also show the next 5 pieces beside the game.

Helper.js 27 Sept 22:37 Trim games to remove extra \r on Windows

On Windows when checking out the code from git some extra \r characters were inserted in games.txt which caused errors in makeMove (undefined:1).
This fix in tetris-helper.js cleans up the games string.
